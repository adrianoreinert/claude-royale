# 👑 Welcome to Claude Royale!

**Claude Royale** is a real-time 1v1 Clash Royale-style battler that runs in your browser — free, no install.

🎮 **Play now:** https://clauderoyale.net
💻 **Open source:** https://github.com/adrianojoi/claude-royale
🕹️ **itch.io page:** https://clauderoyale.itch.io/claude-royale

## Where to start
- 📢 **#announcements** — game news and updates
- 🎮 **#find-a-match** — set up 1v1 matches (use a private room code)
- 🏆 **#clips-and-wins** — share your best plays
- 🐛 **#bug-reports** — found a bug? tell us here
- 💡 **#suggestions** — ideas for cards, modes and improvements
- 🛠️ **#dev-updates** — behind the scenes of development

## How to play with someone here
Open the game in **two tabs** (or two phones) and press ⚔️ Battle in both — or use a **private room** with a 4-letter code.

Have fun and good luck in the arena! 🎉
