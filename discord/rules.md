# 📜 Server rules

To keep the arena a great place for everyone:

1. **Respect above all.** No harassment, hate speech, racism, discrimination or personal attacks.
2. **No spam or flooding.** Don't repeat messages, links or mass mentions.
3. **Right channel for each topic.** Matches in #find-a-match, bugs in #bug-reports, etc.
4. **No NSFW, illegal content or piracy.**
5. **No unsolicited self-promotion.** Advertising other servers/products only with mod permission.
6. **No cheats, exploits or hacks** — and if you find a security flaw, report it privately (security@clauderoyale.net), don't abuse it.
7. **Follow Discord's [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).**

Moderators may remove messages and apply mute/kick/ban as needed. By participating, you agree to these rules.

*Questions or reports: ping a moderator or email hello@clauderoyale.net*
